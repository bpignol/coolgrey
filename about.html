About the Cool Grey project

This github.io website was launched in Berlin at the end of February 2018, as a code learning project, to showcase a series of canvas developed with P5 JS.

In the short atmospheric video above (playing only in desktop view), we follow individuals from the tech community on their quiet strolls through Berlin. It is better experienced with the music produced by Capt.Lovelace, who used to live in Berlin. Discover more on her SoundCloud.

The logo is inspired by the Bauhaus aesthetic, founded nearly 100 years before this project and briefly taught in Berlin. Our design is a tribute to the values promoted by this current, advocating experiments, mixing disciplines, and simplifying forms. Discover more on Bauhaus 100.

The flat-sided typography was a souvenir of the Botanischer Garten metro sign. Discover more on Berlin Typography.

Thanks for having visited us.
